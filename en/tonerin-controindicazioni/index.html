Tonerin Contraindications: Important Safety Info

While Tonerin is a natural supplement with an excellent safety record, certain contraindications should be considered before beginning supplementation.

Who should avoid Tonerin

Individuals with known allergies to olives, vitamin C supplements, or any other ingredient listed on the label should not take Tonerin. Children under 18 and pregnant or breastfeeding women should also avoid use unless specifically advised by a healthcare professional.
